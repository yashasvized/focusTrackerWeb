Delete Your Account

Learn how to permanently delete your Focus Tracker account

⚠️

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, all your cloud data will be permanently removed from our servers. Please read the information below carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you delete your Focus Tracker account, the following actions will be taken:

🗑️ Permanently Deleted

  • Your Google account connection
  • Your premium/subscription status
  • Cloud-synced user profile data
  • Any donation/payment records
  • Authentication credentials

✅ Kept on Device

  • Local tasks and task history
  • App settings and preferences
  • Blocked apps configuration
  • Analytics and streak data
  • Achievements earned

Note: Local data on your device will NOT be automatically deleted. To remove local data, you can uninstall the app or clear app data from your device settings.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to delete your Focus Tracker account from within the app:

  1. Open Focus Tracker
    Launch the Focus Tracker app on your Android device.
  2. Go to Settings
    Tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner of the home screen to open Settings.
  3. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
    Scroll down to find the "Danger Zone" section at the bottom of the Settings page.
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
    Tap on the "Delete Account" option with the red icon.
  5. Confirm Deletion
    Read the warning message carefully, then tap "Delete My Account" to confirm. This action cannot be undone.

Important: You must be signed in to your Google account to delete your account. If you're not signed in, the "Delete Account" option will not be visible.

Can't Access the App?

If you cannot access the Focus Tracker app to delete your account (e.g., you've uninstalled the app or lost access to your device), you can request account deletion by contacting us directly.

Please send an email to our support team with:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your Google account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

After Account Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be signed out of the app automatically
  • Your premium status (if any) will be removed immediately
  • You can continue using the app without an account
  • You can create a new account at any time by signing in again
  • Your previous data and premium status cannot be recovered
