About Focus Tracker

Our Mission

To help people reclaim their focus and achieve their goals by reducing digital distractions and building productive habits.

The Problem We Solve

In today's digital world, we're constantly bombarded with notifications, social media updates, and endless entertainment options. These distractions make it incredibly difficult to focus on what truly matters - whether it's studying for exams, completing work projects, or pursuing personal goals.

Studies show that the average person checks their phone over 100 times a day, and it takes about 23 minutes to regain focus after a distraction. This constant context-switching dramatically reduces our productivity and increases stress.

Our Solution

Focus Tracker takes a simple but effective approach: complete your tasks before you can access distracting apps.

Instead of relying solely on willpower (which is a limited resource), Focus Tracker creates a system that naturally guides you toward productivity:

  • Create your tasks - Define what you need to accomplish
  • Block distractions - Select which apps to block while you have pending tasks
  • Enter Focus Mode - Work in a distraction-free environment
  • Complete and unlock - Finish your tasks to regain access to your apps

This creates a positive feedback loop where completing tasks becomes the key to unlocking your entertainment, making productivity rewarding rather than punishing.
