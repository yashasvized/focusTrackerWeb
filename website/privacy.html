Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Focus Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Focus Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Task Data: Tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, priorities, and completion status.
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences within the app.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous data about how you use the app, such as tasks completed and streaks.
  • App Usage Data: Information about which apps are in the foreground (required for app blocking functionality).
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app compatibility and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Focus Tracker service
  • Block distracting apps when you have pending tasks
  • Track your productivity statistics and streaks
  • Sync your data across devices (if signed in)
  • Send task reminders and notifications
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Provide customer support

Permissions We Request

Focus Tracker requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Accessibility Service: Required to detect which app is currently open and block distracting apps.
  • Usage Access: Required to monitor app usage for blocking functionality.
  • Display Over Other Apps: Required to show the blocking overlay when you try to open a blocked app.
  • Notifications: Required to send task reminders and focus mode notifications.

Important: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any sensitive data accessed through these permissions. All app blocking logic runs locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most of your data (tasks, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Sync: If you sign in with Google, basic account information may be stored securely in Firebase.
  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Focus Tracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage. Subject to Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (non-premium users). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing premium purchases. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

Focus Tracker displays advertisements to non-premium users through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant advertisements.

Premium users who make any purchase will have all advertisements removed permanently.

Children's Privacy

Focus Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@focustracker.app.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide you with the service and for legitimate business purposes. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@focustracker.app
